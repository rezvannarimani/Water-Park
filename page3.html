Rules

1-Children must be supervised by an adult.

2-No lifeguard on duty, use the water park at your own risk.

3-No glass, alcohol or drugs permitted.

4-No running.

5-No animals allowed-guide and assistance dogs accepted.No animals allowed on the wet play area.

6-No bikes, skateboards, scooters or rollerblades to be ridden in this facility.

7-Please leave the waterpark during severe weather.

8-No smoking.

9-No littering-use the bins provided.

10-Do not drink the water within the wet play area, use the taps provided.

11-Do not enter if suffering a contagious illness, skin lesions or if you have had diarrhea in the last 14 days.
